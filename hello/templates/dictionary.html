We present here three dictionaries:
  • Klein Dictionary is based on Ernest Klein's A Comprehensive Etymological Dictionary Of The Hebrew Language. Links between headwords are on the basis of semantic similarity.
  • Clark's Dictionary is based on Matityahu Clark's Etymological Dictionary Of Biblical Hebrew. At the moment, headwords are grouped into phonemic classes and displayed together.
  • Distributional Dictionary is based on the ETCB dataset, and "roots" are linked by distributional criteria.
